About Me

Engineering flight, one system at a time

I'm a Robotics and Automation Engineering graduate and a former member of the DCLab UAV team at the Institute of Technology of Cambodia, where I designed, built, and flew the systems I studied.

My work spans the full stack of a flying machine — from mechanical design and flight controllers to AI-driven autonomy running on Jetson Nano and Raspberry Pi. I'm passionate about advancing UAV technology and eager to apply my skills in a professional setting, collaborate with experts, and innovate in automation and robotics.

When I'm not in the lab, I'm mentoring aspiring engineers and teaching robotics — because the next generation of Cambodian engineers should take off even faster.
